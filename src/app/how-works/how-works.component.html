How it works

This project born as a personal project, in response to migrate music from our facebook linked spotify accounts to a new one not linked. As we could not find any free solution online, we decided to make one, that is how we created 'Janus'.

We connect to spotify through API services, and magicaly migrate your music from your old account to the new one. It is important to mention that we do not store any of your personal information or data, we just connect to spotify and migrate the playlists or songs that you want.

Free

User to user

Made from a spotify user to serve another spotify users.
